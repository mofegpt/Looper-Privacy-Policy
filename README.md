# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:**  01/01/26_

Thank you for using **Looper** (“the App”). Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the App.

---

## Information We Collect

### 1. Photos & Videos

Looper allows users to select videos from their device to practice, analyze, or score music.

- Videos are selected **explicitly by the user**
- The App does **not** access your entire photo library
- Selected videos are used **only within the App**
- Videos are **not uploaded** to our servers

Access to photos or videos is handled using Apple’s system-provided picker, which limits access to only the content you choose.

---

### 2. Usage Data

Looper does **not** collect personal usage data such as:

- Name  
- Email address  
- Phone number  
- Contacts  

---

### 3. Advertising

Looper may display ads using **Google AdMob**.

AdMob may collect:
- Device identifiers
- Approximate location
- Interaction data (such as ad impressions)

This data is used solely to provide and improve advertising services.  
Looper does **not** have access to personally identifiable information collected by AdMob.

For more information about Google’s data practices, visit:  
https://policies.google.com/privacy

---

## How We Use Information

Information is used only to:
- Enable core app functionality (video playback and looping)
- Display ads to support development
- Improve performance and stability

We do **not** sell or share personal data.

---

## Data Storage

- Media selected by the user remains **on the device**
- The App does **not** store videos on external servers
- Preferences and settings are stored **locally** on your device

---

## User Control

You control:
- Which videos you select
- Whether to grant photo access
- Advertising permissions via system settings

You may revoke permissions at any time in:

**Settings → Privacy & Security → Photos**

---

## Children’s Privacy

Looper is not intended for children under the age of 13.  
We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

---

## Third-Party Services

Looper uses third-party services, including:
- **Google AdMob** (advertising)

These services operate under their own privacy policies.

---

## Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time.  
Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
